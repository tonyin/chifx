2014 NFL Conference Championships

  1. Duration: 01/17/14 - 01/19/14
  2. Everyone starts with $1000 fake money
  3. Buy or Sell players at your projected point levels
  4. View your active orders and completed trades under "My Portfolio"
  5. At end of day 01/19/14, we will post up a leaderboard showing who made the most money

Scoring system

QB: touchdown(+4 passing, +6 rushing); 25 passing yds(+1), 10 rushing yds(+1); sack(-1); interception(-2); fumble(-2)


Example for "Buy"

  • I think Russell Wilson will score more than 20 points on Sunday
  • On Russell Wilson's page, I submit a "Buy" order for 200 volume at a point level of 20
  • If someone is already willing to sell Russell Wilson at a point level of 20 or lower, then my order will trade against their order
  • If no one is selling there, then my "Buy" order will be resting in the order book, waiting for someone else to sell against it
  • Settlement: If Russell Wilson puts up 3 passing touchdowns (+12 pts), 300 yds (+12), and 1 interception (-2) for a total of 22 points, I would make (22-20)*200 = $400, assuming all 200 of my "Buy" order traded
  • Settlement: If Russell Wilson only puts up 18 points, I would lose (18-20)*200 = $-400, assuming all 200 of my "Buy" order traded

Example for "Sell"

  • I think Tom Brady will score less than 25 points on Sunday
  • On Tom Brady's page, I submit a "Sell" order for 200 volume at a point level of 25
  • If someone is already willing to buy Tom Brady at a point level of 25 or high, then my order will trade against their order
  • If no one is buying there, then my "Sell" order will be resting in the order book, waiting for someone else to buy against it
  • Settlement: If Tom Brady puts up 1 passing touchdown (+4 pts), 250 yds (+10), and 2 interception (-4) for a total of 10 points, I would make (25-10)*200 = $3000, assuming all 200 of my "Sell" order traded
  • Settlement: If Tom Brady puts up a monster 35 points, I would lose (25-35)*200 = $-2000, assuming all 200 of my "Sell" order traded
