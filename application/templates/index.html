NFL Conference Championships

Our first protoype round was conducted from Jan 12 - Jan 19, 2014. We had 13 people actively trade 4 securities- the QBs from the participating teams. Before the games, our markets were able to trade within 2 points of the final settlement values for three of the four QBs. Highlights include:

  • Sellers Market - Of the 33 trades, 24 won from the short side
  • Securities Liquidity - Peyton Manning traded the most volume, while Russell Wilson traded the least
  • Wilson Misses - Seahawks fans and Wilson supporters got rocked, with Wilson missing pre-market valuation by 10 points

Scoring system

QB: touchdown(+4 passing, +6 rushing); 25 passing yds(+1), 10 rushing yds(+1); 2-pt conv(+2); sack(-1); interception(-2); fumble(-2)
WR: touchdown(+6); 10 yds(+1); 2-pt conv(+2); fumble(-2)
RB: touchdown(+6); 10 yds(+1); 2-pt conv(+2); fumble(-2)
